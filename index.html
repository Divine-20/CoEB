Community outreach program at the Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management (CoEB) in 2023

The Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management (CoEB), engages in educational and awareness-raising initiatives through community outreach programs, aiming to educate the forthcoming generation, encompassing both young individuals and local communities, about the significance of biodiversity and natural resources management. The intention is to underline the critical role environmental protection plays in sustainable development and the enduring conservation of biodiversity, which in turn contributes to global well-being via the Communication and Outreach department of CoEB.

Since the commencement of 2023, the Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management (CoEB) engaged in various outreach activities comprising both Students and local communities as summarized below:
