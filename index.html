Community outreach program at the Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management (CoEB) in 2023

The Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management (CoEB), engages in educational and awareness-raising initiatives through community outreach programs, aiming to educate the forthcoming generation, encompassing both young individuals and local communities, about the significance of biodiversity and natural resources management. The intention is to underline the critical role environmental protection plays in sustainable development and the enduring conservation of biodiversity, which in turn contributes to global well-being via the Communication and Outreach department of CoEB.

Since the commencement of 2023, the Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management (CoEB) engaged in various outreach activities comprising both Students and local communities as summarized below:

6. Community outreach program at Ecole secondaire de butare.

Communication and outreach unit led by Salim Kabano with academic interns from Biology Department from university of Rwanda bwenge benjamin, manasse nishimwe & Niyonshuti ruth, at the Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management reached out to students at Ecole secondaire de butare. conducted community outreach They discussed natural resource management, threats to natural resources as well as impacts to the community and country. An environmental club was officially launched in the school, this new club is a result of education & outreach programs we conducted at the school. After creation of this club, members elected the committee that will be help fulfill their objectives including tree planting in school compound, gardening, participation in outreach programs for local communities, reporting environmental destruction. Members want to know more about the environment near their school including Arboretum of Ruhande and CoEB's activities. This launch will lead to future partnership with this club for environmental conservation.

7. Community outreach program at TVET Bumba secondary school

In a cool and peaceful environment near lake Kivu community outreach program led by Salim Kabano has been extended in Rutsiro District at TVETBumba a school with 5 trades: Electricity, Plumbing, Masonry, Tailoring, Welding. This year's theme is "From agreement to action". After discussions there was formation and launch of an environmental club in the school with the theme "save the environment for future"; they are ready to collaborate with CoEB in their activities. Students shared us their wishes including future field visits to see more about the Centre's activities.

1. Community outreach programs towards local communities

Communication and outreach unit led by Salim Kabano attended community work (umuganda) in huye district, isimbi sector with students from university of Rwanda biology department Nshuti Ruth,Manasse Nishimwe and Bwenge Benjamin who were finalising their academic internship in research and education unit. after the community work students shared with the local communities about wetland management and its related impacts to the community. Talk given by ruth, the other topic was about environmental management and biodiversity conservation talk given by Benjamin, and later the community outreach program was ended by talk given by Manasse entitled water and human health where by local communities were educated more about their surroundings. the guest of honor in the community work was the mayor of huye district.

Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management participated in community work at district level in Huyedistrict and main activity during this activity was prevention of soil erosion through creation of trenches that will help in water catchment. There were discussions with the local communities about natural hazards management and climate change adaptation measures as part of our community outreach program aimed to extend conservation education awareness towards local communities; the team was led by Salim Kabano.

Center of Excellence in Biodiversity and Natural Resource Management has conducted community outreach in Karama sector, Huye district in community gathering (Inteko z'abaturage) where by discussions with local communities were about climate smart agriculture. and later we discussed on minimization of industrial use and engage in advanced utilization of organic fertilizers, agroforestry practices, soil erosion prevention and irrigation practices during prolonged sunshine conditions.
